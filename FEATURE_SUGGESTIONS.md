# Feature Suggestions for CS Hub

## Discovery & Navigation
1. **Advanced filters + sort**: filter by type/engine, then sort by popularity, newest, or A-Z.
2. **Search with typo tolerance**: fuzzy search for game titles and descriptions.
3. **Recently played rail**: pin recently opened games at top for faster return.
4. **Favorites system**: let users star games and view a personal favorites tab.

## Gameplay UX
5. **Fullscreen + quick controls overlay**: show keybinds and one-click fullscreen.
6. **Game status badges**: mark games as "new", "updated", "multiplayer", or "controller-ready".
7. **Lightweight rating signals**: thumbs up/down and play count to guide discovery.
8. **Related games panel**: suggest similar games by type and tags.

## Content & Curation
9. **Tagging system**: add tags like "co-op", "speedrun", "strategy", "relaxing".
10. **Collections/playlists**: curated sets like "5-minute games", "best puzzle games", or "classics".
11. **Seasonal spotlights**: rotating homepage modules for holiday/event picks.
12. **Submission workflow**: simple form to suggest new games, reviewed before publish.

## Reliability & Performance
13. **Broken-link checker**: periodic validation of game URLs and thumbnails.
14. **Lazy-loading thumbnails**: improve homepage speed by loading images on scroll.
15. **Offline-friendly shell**: cache navigation shell and metadata for quick reloads.
16. **Analytics dashboard**: track opens, completion proxies, and retention by category.

## Trust, Safety & Admin
17. **Admin moderation queue**: approve/reject submissions with notes.
18. **Frame compatibility test**: pre-check whether a URL supports embedding.
19. **Content policy flags**: mark age suitability and content notes.
20. **Auto-metadata script**: generate IDs, slugs, and image paths from folder names.
